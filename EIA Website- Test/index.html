Where We Operate

Currently, Amarna Energy is focused on California, a state with one of the largest solar energy markets in the U.S. However, a staggering 95% of solar energy produced in California is wasted due to inefficiencies in the existing power grid.

Our mission is to address this issue by helping households and businesses make the most of their solar energy. Specific neighborhoods with high solar panel adoption and potential for surplus energy sharing include:

  • Santa Monica
  • Pasadena
  • San Diego
  • Fresno
  • Silicon Valley (San Jose area)

These areas have a significant number of solar installations and are ideal for our energy-sharing solutions.

About Our Project

Amarna Energy is dedicated to revolutionizing the way we use and share energy in California. Our project focuses on optimizing solar energy production and facilitating the sharing of surplus energy among community members. By connecting solar energy producers with local consumers, we aim to reduce energy waste, lower costs, and promote sustainable living.

Our Services

  • Solar Energy Production: Helping homeowners and businesses install and maintain solar panels.
  • Energy Sharing Platform: A platform for solar energy producers to share surplus energy with neighbors.
  • Cost Savings: Providing affordable and clean energy solutions to reduce monthly energy bills.
  • Community Engagement: Building a network of environmentally conscious individuals and businesses in California.
